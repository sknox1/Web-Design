My Hero Academia

This is an anime series about a boy name Izuku Midoriya, a boy without a quirk, who dreams about becoming a hero. Izuku inherits a quirk called one for all from a legendary hero. Izuku then goes to a high school with other kids training to become a hero.

Characters

Izuku Midoriya, also known as Deku is mainly who the story is about. His quirk is strength. Along with All Might, the hero who gave his power to Deku. Dekus bully and sometimes friend, Baguko is the hot head in the show. He wants to be the best at everything and will literally do anything it takes. His quirk is explosion. Todoroki, the son of Endeavor is half hot and half cold. He represent the mysterious character who is surpising you.
